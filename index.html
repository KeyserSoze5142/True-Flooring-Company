Our Services

Expert installation across six flooring types — handled with care and craftsmanship from start to finish.

Hardwood Flooring
Classic & Timeless

Hardwood Flooring

Nothing compares to the warmth and lasting beauty of real hardwood. We install both solid and engineered hardwood in a wide range of species, stain colors, and plank widths to perfectly match your home's character.

With proper care, hardwood adds lasting value and can last a lifetime. Our installers ensure tight, seamless results in every room.

  • Solid & engineered hardwood
  • Oak, maple, hickory, walnut & more
  • Nail-down, glue-down & floating methods
  • Sanding, staining & refinishing
  • Subfloor assessment & prep
Get a Free Quote
Luxury Vinyl Plank
Waterproof & Durable

Luxury Vinyl Plank

LVP is one of the most popular flooring choices today — 100% waterproof, incredibly durable, easy to maintain, and available in stunning wood and stone looks that rival the real thing.

Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and households with pets or kids. We carry top-rated brands at competitive prices.

  • 100% waterproof construction
  • Floating & glue-down installation
  • Wide range of wood & stone visuals
  • Pet, child & scratch-resistant options
  • Suitable for all rooms including basements
Get a Free Quote
Tile & Stone
Precision & Style

Tile & Stone

Our tile team delivers flawless results in kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, and outdoor areas. From classic subway tile to large-format porcelain and natural stone, we handle every style with precision.

Proper tile installation starts with preparation — we ensure every surface is leveled and waterproofed before setting the first tile.

  • Porcelain, ceramic, natural stone & mosaic
  • Floor, wall & shower tile installation
  • Large-format & custom pattern layouts
  • Waterproofing for wet areas
  • Grout color selection, matching & sealing
Get a Free Quote
Carpet
Comfort & Warmth

Carpet

Carpet remains the top choice for bedrooms and living areas where comfort and quiet are a priority. We offer a wide variety of styles, textures, and fibers from trusted brands, with professional installation that stretches tightly and lasts for years.

  • Plush, berber, frieze & loop styles
  • Stain-resistant & pet-friendly options
  • Padding selection & installation
  • Stairs, hallways & custom cuts
  • Old carpet removal & disposal available
Get a Free Quote
Laminate
Affordable & Beautiful

Laminate Flooring

Laminate gives you the look of hardwood at a fraction of the cost. Modern laminate is incredibly realistic, highly durable, and simple to maintain — an excellent choice for high-traffic areas and budget-conscious projects.

  • Realistic wood & stone look options
  • Scratch & fade-resistant wear layer
  • Click-lock floating installation
  • AC3 and AC4 commercial-grade options
  • Moisture-resistant underlayment included

About True Flooring

Locally owned and operated, built on craftsmanship, honesty, and a passion for beautiful floors.

Our Story

Rooted in Northport,
Built to Last

True Flooring Company was founded with a clear purpose: to bring expert-level flooring services to homeowners in Northport and the greater Tuscaloosa area — without the franchise price tag or impersonal service that often comes with it.

We're a locally owned and operated business that treats every home the same way we'd treat our own. From the first consultation to the final walk-through, we're committed to honesty, quality, and your complete satisfaction.

Whether you're renovating one room or transforming your entire home, you'll work directly with our experienced team every step of the way. No subcontractors, no surprises, no shortcuts.
